WolfWave is simple to use and mostly runs by itself in the background. Here's how to master its main features.

---

## 🐺 The Menu Bar
The main hub of WolfWave is in your **macOS Menu Bar** (the icons in the top right of your screen). 

- Click the Wolf icon to see what is playing.
- From the menu, you can quickly **Sync Music**, **Check Twitch Chat**, and access **Settings**.

---

## 🎵 Sync Music
**TL;DR: Keep the music flowing.**

Toggle **Sync Music** on in Settings to start tracking Apple Music. WolfWave will instantly detect when you change a song.

<Callout type="info">
  **Pro-tip**: Click the Wolf icon in the Menu Bar to see a quick "Now Playing" card with artwork and artist info.
</Callout>

---

## 📺 Now-Playing Widget
**TL;DR: The easiest widget you've ever set up.**

WolfWave hosts a now-playing widget directly on your Mac. No external links or internet connection needed.

1. Open **Settings → Now-Playing Widget**.
2. Click **Enable Widget Server**.
3. Copy the **Widget Link** (`http://localhost:8766`).
4. In OBS, add a **Browser Source** and paste the Link.
5. Set the size to **500 × 120**.

<Callout type="info">
  **Important**: Turn on "Shutdown source when not visible" in OBS to make sure the widget reconnects smoothly when you switch scenes.
</Callout>

---

## 💬 Twitch Chat Bot
**TL;DR: Chat commands for everyone.**

Once you're logged into Twitch (via **Settings → Twitch**), your bot will automatically connect to your channel when you start the app.

- **Enable Commands**: Pick which commands people can use in chat (like `!song` or `!last`).
- **Cooldowns**: Prevent chat spam by setting "Wait Times" (Cooldowns) for commands.
- **Mod Bypass**: Don't worry, moderators and you can use commands as much as you like.

---

## 🎧 Discord Status
**TL;DR: Show your status.**

When **Discord Status** is enabled, WolfWave will automatically update your Discord profile to show "Listening to Apple Music".

- **Album Art**: High-quality artwork is fetched automatically.
- **Progress Bar**: Shows exactly where you are in the song.
- **No Setup Required**: As long as Discord is open, WolfWave will find it and connect.

---

## ⚙️ Settings
Access **Settings** from the menu bar to:

- **Now-Playing Widget**: Change your widget's port, colors, or fonts.
- **Twitch**: Log in, set commands, or tweak cooldowns.
- **Discord**: Enable or disable the profile status.
- **Advanced**: Check for updates or reset the setup wizard.
