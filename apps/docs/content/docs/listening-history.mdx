<span className="docs-eyebrow">Guide</span>

**Your private play log.** WolfWave can track what you play and turn it into stats. It's off by default, stays only on your Mac, and never gets uploaded.

## Turn it on

**Opt-in, always.** Flip it on in **Settings → History & Stats**, or during the first-launch setup. Until you do, nothing is recorded.

- **Off** means no play log exists.
- **Turn it off later** and you can clear what was saved.

## What counts as a play

**Skipping doesn't count.** A track logs only after it plays past its halfway point or past four minutes, whichever comes first. Hunting through songs won't junk up your history. A song you actually listen to gets logged once.

The log is an on-device, append-only file. It's your data, not a setting, so it's not part of a [settings backup](/docs/backup).

## Stats and charts

Turn history on and **Settings → History & Stats** shows a dashboard (built on SwiftUI Charts):

- **Top artists** by play count.
- **Listening time**, total and recent.
- **7-day trend** of how much you've played.
- **Listening by hour**, so you can see when you listen.
- **Top card** that flips between artists, tracks, and albums.

Two columns on a wide window, one on a narrow one.

## Monthly Wrap

**Your month, wrapped.** Pick any month for a personal, Wrapped-style summary. Save it as a PNG, or send it to Messages, Mail, AirDrop, and anywhere else through the macOS share sheet.

## Retention

**You pick how long to keep it** in **Settings → History & Stats**. Older entries clear at the next launch.

| Keep for | |
|---|---|
| Forever | Nothing is auto-cleared. |
| 7 / 30 / 90 / 180 / 365 days | Entries older than your pick clear at next launch. |

## The !stats chat command

**Viewers can ask for today's top track** with `!stats` in Twitch chat. It only replies while you're live, so it stays quiet off-air. Set custom aliases and a cooldown in **Settings → History & Stats**. Full list in [Bot Commands](/docs/bot-commands).

## Privacy

**Nothing leaves your Mac.** No account, no sync, no upload. Delete your history or turn the feature off and the log is gone.
