<span className="docs-eyebrow">Guide</span>

WolfWave can keep a private log of the tracks you play and turn it into stats. It is off by default, lives only on your Mac, and is never uploaded.

## Turn it on

History is opt-in. Flip it on in **Settings → History & Stats**, or during the first-launch onboarding wizard. Until you do, nothing is recorded.

When it is off, no play log exists. When you turn it off later, you can clear what was saved.

## What counts as a play

A track only counts once it has played past its halfway point, or past four minutes, whichever comes first. So skipping through songs to find one does not pollute your history. A song you actually listen to gets logged once.

The log is an on-device append-only file. It is your data, not a setting, so it is not part of a [settings backup](/docs/backup).

## Stats and charts

With history on, **Settings → History & Stats** shows a dashboard built on SwiftUI Charts:

- **Top artists** by play count.
- **Listening time**, total and recent.
- **A 7-day trend** of how much you have played.
- **A listening-by-hour** breakdown of when you listen.
- **A Top card** that switches between artists, tracks, and albums.

The dashboard is two columns on a wide window and collapses to one on a narrow one.

## Monthly Wrap

Pick any month for a personal, Wrapped-style summary. Save the card as a PNG, or send it straight to Messages, Mail, AirDrop, and anywhere else through the macOS share sheet.

## Retention

Choose how long to keep your history in **Settings → History & Stats**: Forever, or 7, 30, 90, 180, or 365 days. Older entries are cleared at the next launch.

## The !stats chat command

Viewers can ask for today's top track in Twitch chat with `!stats`. It only replies while your stream is live, so it stays quiet off-air. Give it custom aliases and a cooldown in **Settings → History & Stats**. See [Bot Commands](/docs/bot-commands) for the full command list.

## Privacy

Everything here stays on your Mac. There is no account, no sync, and no upload. Deleting your history or turning the feature off removes the log.
