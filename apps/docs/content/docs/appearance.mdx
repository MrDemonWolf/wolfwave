Pick how WolfWave looks. Your choice covers the whole app, the menu bar menu included.

## Pick an appearance

Open **Settings → General** and pick one:

| Option | What it does |
|---|---|
| **System** (default) | Matches macOS, including its light/dark schedule. |
| **Light** | Light look everywhere, even if macOS is in dark mode. |
| **Dark** | Dark look everywhere, even if macOS is in light mode. |

**See it first.** The picker shows System Settings-style preview thumbnails, so you know what each option looks like before you tap it.

## What it changes

**Everything.** The Settings window, the now-playing card, every section, and the menu bar menu. Nothing else to set.

Settings cards sit on a solid macOS surface. They stay readable in both light and dark, no glow, no washout.

## Good to know

- **Pick System** if you want WolfWave to follow your Mac's light/dark schedule.
- **It travels.** Appearance is a preference, so it comes along with [Back Up & Restore](/docs/backup).
