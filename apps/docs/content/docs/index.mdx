# WolfWave

**A tiny menu bar app that connects Apple Music to Twitch, Discord, and your stream overlay.**

Play something in Apple Music. WolfWave handles the rest — chat commands, your Discord profile, and a live overlay all stay in sync. No accounts. No servers. No subscriptions.

---

## What it does

- **Detects every track instantly.** Native Apple integrations, not polling.
- **Answers `!song` in chat.** Title, artist, album — under a second.
- **Updates Discord Rich Presence.** Album art and live progress, just like Spotify.
- **Streams a Now Playing overlay.** Drop into OBS, customize, or build your own.
- **Stays private.** Tokens in macOS Keychain. Sandboxed. Zero telemetry.
