# 🐺 WolfWave

**Share your Apple Music with Twitch, Discord, and beyond.**

WolfWave is a native macOS app that connects your **Apple Music** to **Twitch** chat, **Discord** status, and **now-playing widgets**.

It's fast, lightweight, and works entirely on your Mac — no external servers, no subscriptions, and no complicated setup.

---

### 🚀 30-Second Summary

- **Sync Music**: Instantly detects what's playing in Apple Music.
- **Twitch Chat**: Anyone in chat can type `!song` to see what's playing.
- **Discord Status**: Show what you're listening to on your Discord profile automatically.
- **Now-Playing Widget**: Add a beautiful "Now Playing" overlay to OBS or any browser source.
- **Privacy First**: Your data stays on your Mac. No tracking, no accounts needed.

## Features at a Glance

### 🎵 Music Sync
No "polling" or hacks. WolfWave uses native Apple tech to know exactly when your song changes, the second it happens.

### 💬 Twitch Chat Bot
Built on Twitch's modern API. Fast, reliable chat commands without you lifting a finger.

### 🎧 Discord Status
Show "Listening to Apple Music" on your profile with real-time progress bars and album art. Just like Spotify, but for your library.

### 📺 Now-Playing Widget
No external websites required. WolfWave hosts its own local server for a lag-free, customizable now-playing widget.
