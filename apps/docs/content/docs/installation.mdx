## System Requirements

- **macOS 26.0+** (Tahoe)
- **Apple Silicon** (M1 or later) — Intel Macs are not supported

## Download WolfWave

**Homebrew** (recommended):

```bash
brew tap mrdemonwolf/den
brew install --cask wolfwave
```

**Or** grab the latest `.dmg` from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/MrDemonWolf/WolfWave/releases), open it, and drag WolfWave into Applications.

<Callout type="info">
  WolfWave is signed and notarized by Apple — no Gatekeeper warnings.
</Callout>

## First Launch

WolfWave opens a quick setup wizard on first run:

1. **Music Access** — Click OK when macOS asks to see Apple Music.
2. **Twitch** (optional) — Connect your account to enable `!song` and other chat commands.
3. **Discord** (optional) — Show "Listening to Apple Music" on your profile.
4. **Stream Widget** (optional) — Enable the local server for your OBS browser source.

That's it! WolfWave lives in your **menu bar** — click the icon to see what's playing or open Settings.
