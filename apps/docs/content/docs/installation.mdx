## What you need

- **macOS 26.0+** (Tahoe)
- **Apple Silicon** (M1 or later) — Intel Macs are not supported
- **Apple Music app** — WolfWave only reads from Apple Music (built into macOS). Spotify and other players are not supported.

## Download WolfWave

Two ways to install — the `.dmg` installer for most people, or Homebrew for developers. The download page has both, plus system requirements.

## First launch

WolfWave opens a quick setup wizard on first run:

1. **Music Access** — Click OK when macOS asks to see Apple Music.
2. **Twitch** (optional) — Connect your account to enable `!song`, `!sr`, and other chat commands.
3. **Discord** (optional) — Show "Listening to Apple Music" on your profile.
4. **Stream Widget** (optional) — Turn on the local widget server for your OBS browser source.

That's it. WolfWave lives in your **menu bar** — click the wolf icon at the top of your screen to see what's playing or open Settings.
