## What you need

- **macOS 26.0+** (Tahoe)
- **Apple Silicon** (M1 or later). Intel Macs are not supported
- **Apple Music app**. WolfWave only reads from Apple Music (built into macOS). Spotify and other players are not supported.

## First launch

WolfWave opens an 8-step setup wizard the first time you launch. Skip any optional step and come back later from **Settings**.

<Steps>

<Step>

### Welcome

Quick intro to what WolfWave does and what's about to be set up.

</Step>

<Step>

### Discord *(optional)*

Sign in to your Discord desktop app. WolfWave talks to it over local IPC. No bot token, no server.

</Step>

<Step>

### Twitch *(optional)*

OAuth Device Code flow. You're sent to twitch.tv to approve; tokens land in your macOS Keychain.

</Step>

<Step>

### OBS Widget *(optional)*

Turn on the local widget server, copy the URL (your per-install auth token is baked in), and drop it into OBS as a Browser Source.

</Step>

<Step>

### Preferences

Launch at login, song-change notifications, and other quality-of-life toggles.

</Step>

<Step>

### Apple Music Access

Click *OK* when macOS asks WolfWave to control Apple Music. Required. This is how WolfWave reads the now-playing track.

</Step>

<Step>

### Menu Bar Pointer

A friendly arrow shows where the wolf icon lives. Top-right of your screen.

</Step>

<Step>

### Done

WolfWave is running. Click the wolf icon any time to see what's playing or open **Settings**.

</Step>

</Steps>
