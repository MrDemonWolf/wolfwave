WolfWave is designed to be the only tool you need to bridge your Apple Music with Twitch, Discord, and now-playing widgets.

---

## 🎵 Music Sync
**TL;DR: WolfWave sees what you hear, instantly.**

- **Native Sync**: Uses macOS ScriptingBridge for direct access to Apple Music. No hacks, no lag.
- **Set and Forget**: Once enabled, it runs silently in your menu bar.
- **Smart History**: Remembers your `!last` song so you don't have to.
- **Power Saver**: Automatically slows down during Low Power Mode to save your MacBook's battery.

---

## 💬 Twitch Chat Bot
**TL;DR: Chat commands on autopilot.**

- **Modern API**: Built on Twitch EventSub for lightning-fast responses.
- **Custom Commands**: Enable `!song`, `!last`, and more with one click.
- **Anti-Spam**: Built-in cooldowns so your chat doesn't get cluttered.
- **Mod Friendly**: Mods and the channel owner bypass all cooldowns automatically.

---

## 🎧 Discord Status
**TL;DR: Show off your taste.**

- **Live Profile**: Shows "Listening to Apple Music" on your Discord profile.
- **Auto Art**: Fetches high-quality album covers automatically via iTunes.
- **Progress Bars**: Shows your friends exactly how far into the track you are.
- **Zero Setup**: Connects via Discord's local socket. No bot tokens required.

---

## 📺 Now-Playing Widget
**TL;DR: A beautiful widget in seconds.**

- **No Internet Needed**: The widget is hosted locally on your Mac. It works even if the cloud is down.
- **OBS Ready**: Add as a Browser Source in OBS, or open in any browser.
- **Real-time**: Updates the millisecond the song changes.
- **Customizable**: Tweak colors, fonts, and layout to match your style.

---

## 🛡️ Security & Privacy
**TL;DR: Your data is your business.**

- **Keychain Secure**: Your Twitch tokens are stored in the macOS Keychain, the same place your passwords live.
- **No Tracking**: WolfWave doesn't track what you listen to or send your data to any servers.
- **Signed & Notarized**: Fully verified by Apple so you can install with confidence.
